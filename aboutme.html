About Me

I am inspired by other young women in fashion who’ve come before me. My favorite models are Tanya Dziahileva, Shalom Harlow, and Lindsay Wixon.

My favorite fashion is vintage from the 50’s and 60’s. My favorite books are My Year of Rest and Relaxation by Ottessa Moshfegh, and The Virgin Suicides by Jeffrey Eugenides. These books are very meaningful and poetic. My favorite movie is Spring Breakers. I loved the cinematic style of the movie and the way it was filmed.
