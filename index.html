Kai Schreiber

My name is Kai Schreiber, I’m 16 years old. I am a model, and have started recently in September 2024. So far, I have shot a Valentino campaign, walked a Valentino fashion show in Paris, shot for Interview Magazine, and shot for Harper‘s Bazaar magazine.

Once school ends, I’ll be flying back to Paris. Hopefully, I’ll be walking for Celine, Max Mara, and others. I’ve wanted to model since I was very young, so when I got scouted, it was very exciting for me. This is just the beginning and I cannot wait to see more that my career brings.
